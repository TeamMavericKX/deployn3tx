# DeployNet Roadmap

## Vision Statement

DeployNet aims to revolutionize content delivery by creating a truly decentralized, cost-free alternative to traditional CDNs. Our mission is to make global content distribution accessible to everyone, from individual developers to large enterprises, while preserving privacy and reducing infrastructure costs.

## Strategic Goals

### 2026 Goals (Year 1)
- **Q1**: Complete MVP with basic P2P functionality
- **Q2**: Achieve 50% content delivery via P2P in controlled environments
- **Q3**: Onboard 100+ early adopter websites
- **Q4**: Achieve feature parity with basic CDN services

### 2027 Goals (Year 2)
- **Q1**: Launch enterprise offering with SLA
- **Q2**: Implement advanced routing algorithms
- **Q3**: Introduce blockchain-based incentive system
- **Q4**: Achieve 70% P2P content delivery rate

### 2028+ Long-term Vision
- Global scale with millions of active peers
- Advanced edge computing capabilities
- AI-powered content prediction and caching
- Integration with Web3 ecosystems
